Questions

Good to know
before we talk.

Straight answers to what people ask first. Don't see yours? Just ask →

How do you charge?

Fixed, flat fees quoted up front after your free consultation — no surprise hourly bills. You'll know the price before any work starts.

Do you work remotely or in person?

Both. Most work happens securely online so we can serve clients wherever they are, with in-person available by appointment.

What kinds of businesses do you work with?

From owner‑operated small businesses to larger finance teams — across services, trades, and professional practices. The accounting and tax work suits smaller businesses; the risk and audit advisory scales to bigger ones too. Not sure you're a fit? Ask — we'll tell you straight.

What accounting software do you use?

We work in the tools you already use (QuickBooks, Xero, and others) and add light automation for the routine — reconciliations, deadlines, and invoicing.

I'm behind on my books or taxes. Can you still help?

Yes — catch-up bookkeeping, cleanup, and back-filing are common starting points. We'll get you current and keep you there.

How do we get started?

Book a free 30-minute consultation. We'll talk through your situation and follow up with a clear written scope and a fixed quote.
