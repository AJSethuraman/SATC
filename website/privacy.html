This Privacy Policy explains how Sethuraman Accounting, Tax & Consulting (“SATC,” “we,” “us”) handles information when you visit satcllp.com or contact us through it.

Information we collect

  • Information you provide. If you submit our contact form or email us, we receive the details you choose to share — typically your name, email address, and message.
  • Scheduling information. If you book a consultation, our scheduling provider (e.g., Calendly) collects the details needed to book the meeting, subject to its own privacy policy.
  • Usage data. We may use privacy-friendly analytics to understand aggregate, non-identifying traffic (such as page views and referrers). We do not use this to build advertising profiles.

How we use it

  • To respond to your inquiry and provide the services you request.
  • To schedule and conduct consultations.
  • To understand and improve how the site is used.
  • To meet legal, tax, and professional obligations.

How we share it

We do not sell your personal information. We share it only with service providers that help us operate — for example, our scheduling provider, form/email provider, analytics provider, and website host — and where required by law or to protect our rights. Client engagements are additionally governed by our professional confidentiality obligations and your engagement agreement.

Cookies & analytics

The core site does not require advertising cookies. Embedded tools (such as the scheduler) and any analytics may set their own cookies or use similar technologies; these are governed by their providers’ policies.

Data retention

We keep inquiry and client information only as long as needed for the purposes above and to meet legal and professional record-keeping requirements, then delete or de-identify it.
