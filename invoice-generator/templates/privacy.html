Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 21, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how SATC LLP ("SATC," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use Invoicer (the "Service"). Questions? Email arjun_sethuraman@satcllp.com.

Information we collect

  • Account information: your email address and a securely hashed password.
  • Business profile: your business name, email, address, tax ID, and logo, which appear on the invoices you create.
  • Invoice and client data: invoice details and any client information you enter, such as names, addresses, emails, line items, and amounts.
  • Payment connection: if you connect Stripe, we store your Stripe account identifier and whether it can accept charges. We never receive or store your customers' full card numbers.
  • Technical data: basic logs such as IP address, browser type, and timestamps, used for security, debugging, and reliability.
  • Cookies: a single session cookie that keeps you logged in and protects forms. We do not use third-party advertising cookies.

How we use information

We use your information to provide and operate the Service: creating and storing your invoices, generating PDFs, sending the emails you request, creating Stripe payment links and checkouts, securing your account, and maintaining and improving the Service.

Payment information

Online card payments are processed by Stripe. We do not collect or store card numbers — that information goes directly to Stripe. See Stripe's Privacy Policy.

Address suggestions

When you use the address-search field, the text you type is sent to a third-party, OpenStreetMap-based address-suggestion service (Photon) to return matching addresses. It runs in your browser and is used only to help you fill in addresses faster.

How we share information

We share information only with service providers that help us run the Service — including Stripe (payments), our hosting provider, and our email-delivery provider — each acting on our behalf. We may disclose information if required by law or to protect rights, property, or safety. We do not sell your personal information.

Data retention

We keep your information while your account is active. When you delete your account (Account → Security), your account and its invoices are permanently removed from our database. Limited technical logs or backups may persist for a short period before being overwritten.

Your choices and rights

You can view, edit, or delete your invoices, update your business profile, roll your API key, and delete your account at any time from within the Service. Depending on where you live (for example, under the EU/UK GDPR or California's CCPA), you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or port your data, or to object to certain processing. Contact us to exercise these rights.

Security

We hash passwords, serve the Service over HTTPS, and limit access to your data. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we work to protect your information.

Children

The Service is not directed to children under 16, and we do not knowingly collect their personal information.

International users

We operate the Service in the United States. If you use it from elsewhere, your information may be processed in the United States.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will revise the effective date above and, for material changes, provide notice where appropriate.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email arjun_sethuraman@satcllp.com.
